Product Details

In 2019, pharmaceutical and biotechnological industries registered a moderate pay gap (9,9%) in favor of men, who are more employed in these companies than women. However, it is worth to point out that the gender pay gap in this type of societies is below the average in the process industry sector (4,4%).
